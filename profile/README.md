<p><em>Troll 2</em>&nbsp;is a 2025 Norwegian monster film directed by&nbsp;<strong>Roar Uthaug</strong>&nbsp;and written by&nbsp;<strong>Espen Aukan</strong>. It is produced by&nbsp;<strong>Espen Horn</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>Kristian Strand Sinkerud</strong>&nbsp;under Motion Blur, with Netflix as the distributor. The film is the largest production ever in the Nordic countries. It was shot in Norway and Budapest, combining breathtaking landscapes with high-end visual effects. The movie continues the story of Nora, Andreas, and Captain Kris as they face a new troll threat.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Plot</h2>
<p>The film begins with peace returning after the events of the first movie. But a new troll awakens deep in the mountains. This creature is larger, stronger, and more dangerous. The Norwegian armed forces prepare with advanced technology, including UV light-powered machines. Nora Tidemann, Andreas Isaksen, and Captain Kristoffer Holm return to lead the mission.</p>
<p>The team faces challenges as the troll moves closer to populated areas. The story mixes suspense, action, and emotional drama. Nora struggles with her past and her role as a leader. Andreas brings humor and humanity to the tense mission. Captain Kris shows courage and discipline as the military fights the colossal threat. The climax is a massive showdown between humans and the troll, testing courage, unity, and sacrifice.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Final Words</h2>
